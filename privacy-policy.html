Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Currency Converter (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and compliance with applicable regulations.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services beyond AdMob. No user data is ever shared externally.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

5. User Rights

No personal data is collected; therefore, data access or deletion rights under GDPR/CCPA do not apply.
